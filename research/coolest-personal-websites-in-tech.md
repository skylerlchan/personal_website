# Coolest Personal Websites in Tech

A curated collection of the most impressive, creative, and inspiring personal websites from developers, designers, engineers, and founders in the tech industry.

---

## Interactive / 3D Sites

### 1. Bruno Simon -- Creative Developer
- **URL:** [bruno-simon.com](https://bruno-simon.com/)
- **What makes it notable:** Arguably the most famous developer portfolio on the internet. Visitors navigate a full 3D world by driving a toy car around to explore his work. Built with **Three.js** (supporting both WebGL and WebGPU via TSL), **Rapier** physics engine, and **Howler.js** for audio. Includes a racing circuit, hidden achievements, and custom-composed music. Fully open-source under MIT license. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

### 2. Jesse Zhou -- Web Developer & Digital Artist
- **URL:** [jesse-zhou.com](https://jesse-zhou.com/)
- **What makes it notable:** A 3D interactive portfolio themed as "Jesse's Ramen" -- visitors are greeted with a cooking-themed loading screen before entering an immersive environment built with **Three.js** and **Blender**. The entire experience is a playful metaphor where you "cook" ramen to explore his work. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

### 3. Keita Yamada -- Designer & Developer
- **URL:** [p5aholic.me](https://p5aholic.me/)
- **What makes it notable:** A Japanese designer/developer whose site is a technical showcase of advanced **WebGL**, **GLSL shaders**, **GSAP animations**, and **three.js**. The animated background serves as a live demonstration of his capabilities. Won an **Awwwards Site of the Day** nomination. Minimalist text-focused layout lets the WebGL work speak for itself. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

### 4. Christian Kaisermann -- Developer
- **URL:** [kaisermann.me](https://kaisermann.me/)
- **What makes it notable:** Features a unique retro TV-channel switching interface where visitors flip through "channels" to explore different sections of his portfolio. A highly memorable interaction pattern that showcases strong coding skills in an unexpected way. ([HubSpot](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/best-personal-websites))

## Beautifully Minimal / Clean Sites

### 5. Brittany Chiang -- Senior Frontend Engineer, Klaviyo
- **URL:** [brittanychiang.com](https://brittanychiang.com/)
- **What makes it notable:** The gold standard for clean developer portfolios. Dark background with light elements, sticky sidebar navigation, and meticulous attention to **web accessibility** (her specialty). Built with **Next.js** and **Tailwind CSS**, designed in **Figma**, deployed on **Vercel**. Previously worked at Apple and Upstatement. Includes playful Easter eggs like a Doctor Who Tardis gif. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

### 6. Lee Robinson -- Developer Advocate (formerly Vercel, now Cursor)
- **URL:** [leerob.com](https://leerob.com/)
- **What makes it notable:** A masterclass in minimalism. Text-focused layout with zero clutter, sophisticated dark mode with system preference detection, and **variable fonts** (Stix Two Text). Built with **Next.js** and React. Prioritizes his best writing and social connections. Embodies his philosophy: "make technology easy to understand." 15 years of coding experience distilled into pure simplicity.

### 7. Maxime Bonhomme -- Full-Stack Developer & Former Tech Lead
- **URL:** [bonhomme.lol](https://bonhomme.lol/)
- **What makes it notable:** Ultra-minimalist interface that emphasizes text and white space. Demonstrates that sometimes the most impressive design choice is restraint. The `.lol` domain adds personality. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

## Creative Portfolio Sites

### 8. Adenekan Wonderful -- Frontend Developer & UX Architect
- **URL:** [codewonders.dev](https://www.codewonders.dev/)
- **What makes it notable:** Features a striking **glitch/noise animation effect** on hero text using clip-path animations and pseudo-elements. Custom "Neuriat Grotesk" typography, animated gradient backgrounds with grid pattern overlays, and an elaborate **custom cursor system** with tracking animations. Dark theme optimized with CSS custom properties. Balances minimalism with technical sophistication.

### 9. Cyd Stumpel -- Creative Developer & Educator, Amsterdam
- **URL:** [cydstumpel.nl](https://cydstumpel.nl/)
- **What makes it notable:** An **award-winning** portfolio from a creative developer who teaches at Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. Specializes in motion-heavy, animation-rich websites. Portfolio includes projects like "De Visdeurbel" and "Ray Charles Video Museum." Blog covers advanced CSS techniques, **scroll-driven animations**, and **view transitions**. Speaks at conferences on design, code, and accessibility.

### 10. Olaolu Olawuyi -- Staff Software Engineer, Shopify
- **URL:** [olaolu.dev](https://olaolu.dev/)
- **What makes it notable:** Vibrant color palette with clear skill descriptions from a nearly 15-year veteran of web engineering. Clean, structured design with custom font loading optimization (`font-display: swap`), structured schema markup for SEO, and streamlined three-section navigation. Reflects deep expertise in performance optimization and scalable systems. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

### 11. Adham Dannaway -- Product Designer & Frontend Developer
- **URL:** [adhamdannaway.com](https://www.adhamdannaway.com/)
- **What makes it notable:** Eye-catching split-screen hero image that literally shows half designer / half developer, perfectly illustrating his dual expertise. Strong graphics-first approach that immediately communicates what he does. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

## Developer Blogs & Content-Driven Sites

### 12. Josh W. Comeau -- Web Developer & Educator
- **URL:** [joshwcomeau.com](https://www.joshwcomeau.com/)
- **What makes it notable:** One of the most beloved developer blogs on the web. Features elaborate **SVG wave animations**, layered background elements creating depth, and sophisticated dark mode with CSS variables. The site itself is a living portfolio of the techniques he teaches. Creator of popular courses: "CSS for JS Developers," "The Joy of React," and "Whimsical Animations." Every tutorial features custom interactive widgets and visualizations.

### 13. Cassidy Williams -- Senior Director of Developer Advocacy, GitHub
- **URL:** [cassidoo.co](https://cassidoo.co/)
- **What makes it notable:** Features **randomized accent colors** (green, pink, blue, yellow) that shift on hover interactions, creating a playful, dynamic experience on every visit. Clean and approachable layout from one of tech's most recognizable voices. Also a startup advisor, investor, and open source contributor based in Chicago.

### 14. Robin Wieruch -- Freelance Web Developer & Educator
- **URL:** [robinwieruch.de](https://www.robinwieruch.de/)
- **What makes it notable:** A content powerhouse with ~50,000 subscribers. Purple accent color (#823eb7) throughout, clean uppercase headers with generous letter-spacing, and fixed sidebar share buttons. Author of "The Road to React" and "The Road to Next" courses. Prioritizes content and readability above all else.

## Unique & Personality-Driven Sites

### 15. Hugo Bazin -- Product Designer
- **URL:** [hugobazin.com](https://www.hugobazin.com/)
- **What makes it notable:** Combines warmth and personality with professional polish. Features a **sticky PDF resume download button**, selective emoji use for personality, and collapsible experience sections. Strikes the rare balance of being friendly and engaging while remaining professional. ([HubSpot](https://blog.hubspot.com/marketing/best-personal-websites))

### 16. Charles Bruyerre -- Frontend Developer & Graphic Designer
- **URL:** [itssharl.ee/fr](https://itssharl.ee/fr)
- **What makes it notable:** Interactive animated background that gives the site a modern, playful feel. The custom domain (itssharl.ee) is clever branding. Demonstrates both development and graphic design skills simultaneously. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

### 17. Tamal Sen -- Software Engineer
- **URL:** [tamalsen.dev](https://tamalsen.dev/)
- **What makes it notable:** Dark theme designed to **mimic an IDE/code editor**, immediately signaling to visitors that this is a developer's space. Uses project screenshots effectively and creates a familiar environment for technical recruiters and fellow developers. ([Hostinger](https://www.hostinger.com/tutorials/web-developer-portfolio))

### 18. Gloria Lo -- Senior Product Designer, Canva
- **URL:** [glorialo.design](https://glorialo.design/)
- **What makes it notable:** Comprehensive UX case studies with clear, structured sections showing her design process. Includes a "Play" section for personal creative work (music and art), showing dimension beyond the professional. Currently at Canva. ([Site Builder Report](https://www.sitebuilderreport.com/inspiration/personal-websites))
